# Initial User Research Survey

## Purpose

This survey was created to understand how students currently manage assignments, deadlines and revision in order to inform the design of the AI Study Planner.

10 July 2026

- College students
- University students
- Apprentices
- Adult learners

## Questions Covered

- Current methods of tracking assignments
- Study habits
- Biggest challenges
- Feature preferences
- Preferred platform
- Interest in testing the application
